Portfolio · AI Voice Companion

Marvin 一隻活在 Discord 語音頻道裡的陪伴型 AI。它記得每一個人、會放歌、會跟你一搭一唱、看得到你的螢幕——而且它換過兩次名字。這是它三個月內從玩具長成平台的故事。 A companion AI that lives inside a Discord voice channel. It remembers everyone, plays music, riffs back and forth with you, and can see your screen — and it changed its name twice. This is how it grew from a toy into a platform in three months.

Simon→Suki→Marvin 2026.03 — now
The Lineage · 三個名字The Lineage · Three Names

它忘記過自己叫什麼，兩次It forgot its own name. Twice.

這隻 bot 原本是一個台灣電競小團體的私人玩具。整套 Simon → Suki → Marvin 的更迭，壓縮在 2026 年 3 月到 4 月中、大約六週內。每次改名都在程式碼底層留下一塊沒被清掉的化石。

This bot started as a private toy for a small Taiwanese gaming crew. The whole Simon → Suki → Marvin lineage compressed into roughly six weeks, from March to mid-April 2026. Every rename left a fossil buried in the codebase that nobody ever cleaned up.

Simon

2026 年 3 月 1 日March 1, 2026 origin

原型。最早的版本，那群人口中的「Agent Simon」。它的誕生只活在私人聊天記錄裡，沒進任何系統 log。到了四月底，朋友們已經在講它「快要走到生命盡頭」。

The prototype. The earliest build — "Agent Simon" to the crew. Its birth survives only in private chat logs, in no system log at all. By late April, friends were already saying it was "about to reach the end of its life."

化石：python 虛擬環境至今仍叫 venv_simon/ — 改名時沒人重建它，像奠基石一樣留到今天。
Fossil: the Python virtualenv is still named venv_simon/ — never rebuilt on rename, left standing like a foundation stone.

Suki

2026 年 4 月初early April 2026 the soul

極短命，卻是靈魂誕生的年代。Suki 時期，bot 第一次長出記憶、人格、對每個人的偏見與印象。名字後來死了，但這套系統的命名永生。

Short-lived, but the era the soul was born. Under Suki, the bot first grew memory, personality, and its own biases and impressions of each person. The name died; the system named after it is immortal.

化石：suki_memory.json · suki_miner.py · suki_budget.py · DB 欄位 suki_impression（"Marvin's inner monologue about this person"）。整套記憶系統至今仍以她為名。
Fossil: suki_memory.json · suki_miner.py · suki_budget.py · DB field suki_impression ("Marvin's inner monologue about this person"). The whole memory system still carries her name.

Marvin

2026 年 4 月中 — 至今mid-April 2026 — now current

現在的樣子。名字取自《銀河便車指南》那隻憂鬱機器人——而它的厭世人格在改名第一天就在了。2026 年 5 月 7 日以 marvin-voice-core 開源。

The current form. Named after the depressed robot from The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy — and the deadpan, world-weary persona was there from day one. Open-sourced as marvin-voice-core on May 7, 2026.

程式啟動字串：Marvin Bot (Cog Edition) Initialized — 自最早的 log（2026-04-25）起就是這一行。
Boot string: Marvin Bot (Cog Edition) Initialized — unchanged since the earliest log (2026-04-25).

The Claude Era · 每週更新重點The Claude Era · Weekly cadence

開源之後，幾乎每週長出一套新系統After open-sourcing, a new system almost every week

5 月 7 日開源、開發節奏切到與 Claude 協作之後，速度從「偶爾加個功能」變成「一週一套系統」。下面是 git 釘得死的每週重點——不是計畫，是實際 commit 出來的東西。

After open-sourcing on May 7 and shifting the workflow to pairing with Claude, the pace went from "an occasional feature" to "a system a week." Below are the git-pinned weekly highlights — not a roadmap, but what actually got committed.

2026 · 05 / 11–17 · 第 1 週Week 1
遊戲化 + 記憶骨架Games + the memory backbone
猜謎遊戲 Busted 上線（語音整合＋音效）。語音記憶系統四層全實作（把時間壓縮成 prompt）＋實時向量索引＋懶執行壓縮。Apple 平台全停 Whisper，改 Swift-only STT。語音資料 opt-in consent、TTS 風暴保護、Companion app bridge。
The Busted quiz game ships (voice + SFX). The voice-memory system gets all four layers (compressing time into prompts) plus a real-time vector index and lazy compression. Apple platforms drop Whisper entirely for Swift-only STT. Opt-in voice consent, TTS storm protection, the Companion app bridge.
2026 · 05 / 18–24 · 第 2 週Week 2
海龜湯推理 + 意圖硬化Lateral-thinking puzzles + intent hardening
最重的一週（單週近百 commit）。海龜湯推理遊戲整套：主動提示、LLM 維度遞進的 hint generator、HintNode 編織網、個人化 hint 排序（資訊增益選法）。VAD 遊戲模式 RMS bump；點歌改 YouTube Music 優先；一連串意圖修復（強弱訊號 fast-track、wake gate、pre_filter 去噪、silent failure 改 warning 不吞意圖）。
The heaviest week (nearly 100 commits). The full Turtle Soup lateral-thinking game: proactive hints, an LLM hint generator that escalates by dimension, a HintNode graph, personalized hint ranking by information gain. A game-mode VAD RMS bump; music now hits YouTube Music first; a long run of intent fixes (strong/weak fast-track, wake gates, pre_filter denoising, silent failures turned into warnings instead of swallowed intents).
2026 · 05 / 25–31 · 第 3 週Week 3
Social Catalyst — 它開始「主動」Social Catalyst — it starts speaking first
從「被叫才答」進化到「主動發話」。SpeakBus 主動發話架構＋ SpeakerTopicGraph ＋ RoomMoodState。DuckingAgent 偵測兩人熱聊就自己讓位；ProactiveTopicAgent 冷場拋話題；MemoryCallbackAgent 主題返場（「你之前說要⋯」）；MoodAgent 三軸心情、BridgeAgent 跨人串話。點歌改 Gemini grounded search 取代盲猜。
From "answers when called" to "speaks first." The SpeakBus proactive-speech architecture plus a SpeakerTopicGraph and RoomMoodState. DuckingAgent backs off when two people are mid-conversation; ProactiveTopicAgent floats a topic when the room goes quiet; MemoryCallbackAgent brings back old threads ("you said you'd…"); a three-axis MoodAgent and a cross-person BridgeAgent. Song lookup switches to Gemini grounded search instead of blind guessing.
2026 · 06 / 01–07 · 第 4 週Week 4
本地混音台 + Marmo 一搭一唱 + 治理A local mixer + Marmo the duo + governance
Plan 12 本地混音台（f32 on-demand mixer，每首歌響度正規化、按鈕步進）。Marmo 登場——Marvin 的雙人搭檔：DualSpeakAgent 雙人對白、自發漫才（ツッコミ 公式、緊張緩和）、刀子嘴豆腐心對上 Marvin 的冷淡。冷場直接拋話題（移除「要我出話題嗎」確認）。後段轉向治理與韌性：LLM 池歸因、daily-review 雙層 silent-failure 防護、遊戲知識查詢 agent、跨 coding agent 的記憶導出。
Plan 12, a local audio mixer (an f32 on-demand mixer with per-song loudness normalization and stepped volume). Marmo arrives — Marvin's comedy partner: a DualSpeakAgent for two-voice dialogue, spontaneous manzai (the tsukkomi formula, tension-and-release), a tough-love foil against Marvin's deadpan. The room going quiet now triggers a topic directly (the "want a topic?" confirmation is gone). The back half turns to governance and resilience: LLM-pool attribution, two-layer silent-failure guards on the daily review, a game-knowledge agent, and a cross-agent memory export.
